Helga Baumgarten

Widerstand in Palästina: Was wollen Hamas und Fatah?

The Middle East conflict remains one of the most pressing geopolitical issues of our time. In this in-depth work, Helga Baumgarten analyzes the historical roots, the political developments and the dynamics of occupation and resistance in Palestine. With an expanded and updated content, the book offers an in-depth classification of current events as well as possible perspectives for the future. An indispensable contribution for anyone who wants to understand the background to this conflict.
