The Last Days of Granada

“The Last Days of Granada“ is an eyewitness account written by an anonymous Muslim author and soldier, translated into English for the first time. It provides a fascinating glimpse into the final chapter of Muslim reign in Andalusia. Its literary qualities and historical insights make this a significant work that deserves to be studied for its portrayal of a pivotal moment in history.
