Helga Baumgarten

Hamas: Der politische Islam in Palästina

In her groundbreaking work, originally published in 2006, renowned journalist, historian and Middle East scholar Helga Baumgarten delves deep into the intricacies of Hamas, offering a comprehensive and insightful examination that remains as relevant today as when it was first published. By placing Hamas in its broader socio-political context, Baumgarten illuminates the complexities and challenges facing Palestinian society, as well as the broader dynamics shaping the region.
