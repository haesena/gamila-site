Helga Baumgarten

Palästina: Befreiung in den Staat

Palästina: Befreiung in den Staat by Helga Baumgarten is now published in a slightly revised new edition. The work, which illustrates the Palestinians' decades-long struggle for freedom and national self-determination, offers a precise analysis of the political, social and historical developments that led to the emergence of Palestinian identity. Baumgarten's in-depth knowledge makes this book an indispensable contribution to the discussion on the Middle East conflict and its future.
